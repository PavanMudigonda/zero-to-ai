5. **Share your work** on GitHub/LinkedIn

---

## 💡 Lab Best Practices

**Before Starting:**
- ✅ Review the corresponding theory in main curriculum
- ✅ Set up your development environment
- ✅ Allocate dedicated practice time (2-3 hours minimum)

**While Working:**
- ✅ Type code yourself (don't just copy-paste)
- ✅ Experiment with variations
- ✅ Take notes on what you learn
- ✅ Ask questions in course discussions

**After Completing:**
- ✅ Review what you learned
- ✅ Build a variation or extension
- ✅ Add to your portfolio
- ✅ Share your experience
